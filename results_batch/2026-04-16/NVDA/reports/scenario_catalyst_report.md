## Scenario Summary: NVDA

The company’s TTM revenue of about $216B, operating margin near 65%, profit margin above 55%, ROE above 100%, and large free cash flow base support the AI infrastructure leadership thesis. The main tension is valuation and cyclicality: the stock trades at a very large market cap of roughly $4.8T, a high P/B near 30.6x, and beta above 2.3, making the equity highly sensitive to AI capex expectations, gross margin durability, export restrictions, and rates.

Base case probability is highest: AI/data-center demand remains strong enough to support forward EPS expectations, but the stock needs fresh earnings confirmation to break out after a sideways period. Bull case requires upward revisions, sustained hyperscaler capex, and evidence that newer growth vectors such as robotics, industrial AI, and quantum/Ising AI are becoming material. Bear case centers on multiple compression, margin normalization, export/policy shocks, or signs that customer AI capex is peaking.
