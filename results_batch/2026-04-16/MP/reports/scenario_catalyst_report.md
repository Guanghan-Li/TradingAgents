## MP Scenario Summary

MP is a high-beta, execution-driven rare-earth and magnet story. The setup is strategically attractive, but the current fundamentals are still weak: TTM EPS is `-0.50`, EBITDA is negative, free cash flow is about `-$237M`, and the stock is already discounting a large improvement with forward EPS of `1.31` and forward P/E near `47x`. That leaves MP most sensitive to one question: can downstream magnet scaling convert policy tailwinds and supply-chain demand into durable margins and cash generation?

- **Bull case: 30%**. MP shows that magnet production is scaling on time, demand visibility improves, and Q1/FY26 commentary supports a real earnings and cash-flow inflection. In that outcome, MP keeps a strategic premium and can re-rate on forward industrial-policy leverage rather than trailing losses.
- **Base case: 45%**. The strategic narrative holds, but proof points arrive unevenly. Q1 2026 results on **May 7, 2026** likely matter more for guidance and ramp commentary than headline earnings. MP can stay premium-valued, but the stock likely remains volatile and range-bound until EBITDA and FCF visibly inflect.
- **Bear case: 25%**. Magnet ramp or customer conversion lags, cash burn stays heavy, or management pushes out profitability. With trailing economics still weak, MP would be exposed to multiple compression if the market stops underwriting a near-term earnings step-up.

**Key signposts for MP**

- Q1 2026 results on **May 7, 2026**: magnet volume, revenue mix, EBITDA trend, free-cash-flow burn, and management commentary on FY26 ramp timing.
- Evidence that MP is moving from strategic story to operating leverage story: higher downstream contribution, better margins, and clearer customer offtake.
- Macro/rates sensitivity: hotter inflation or a more hawkish Fed can pressure long-duration, premium-multiple names like MP even if the company-specific narrative stays intact.

**Thesis invalidation logic for MP**

- Bull case is invalidated if May guidance shows continued heavy burn with no credible timeline for magnet scale economics.
- Base case is invalidated positively if MP proves a faster-than-expected profitability inflection, or negatively if execution slips enough that the forward earnings bridge loses credibility.
- Bear case is invalidated if MP demonstrates sustained downstream conversion, improving margins, and narrowing cash burn quickly enough to justify the premium multiple.
