Neutral is the correct relative stance because MP sits between improved tape and unproven operations. The most likely Base case explicitly points to volatile, range-bound trading until profitability and free-cash-flow conversion become clearer. Overweight would require the 2026-05-07 earnings release to show a credible operating bridge: magnet ramp progress, customer qualification, better margin trajectory, and visibly narrowing cash burn. Underweight would require operational disappointment or technical failure: guidance that pushes out profitability, continued heavy cash burn, or a decisive loss of the 59.5 to 60.6 support zone and the reclaimed 200-day area near 61.66. Until that evidence arrives, the decisive committee conclusion for MP is Hold with a Neutral stance.
