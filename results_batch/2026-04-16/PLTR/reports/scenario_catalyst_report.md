## PLTR Scenario Summary
PLTR screens as a high-quality, high-duration software/defense asset with unusually strong profitability for its cohort, but the stock is priced for sustained near-flawless execution. The fundamental backdrop is strong: about 36.3% net margin, 40.9% operating margin, roughly $1.26B in free cash flow, and strong liquidity. The constraint is valuation: about 226.6x TTM P/E, 76.7x forward P/E, 46.2x price/book, and roughly 76x trailing sales by market cap/revenue. That leaves PLTR very sensitive to growth durability, large-contract timing, competitive AI narratives, and rate repricing.

**Bull 35%**: AIP adoption and defense/intelligence wins keep growth and margin expansion strong enough to support the scarcity premium.

**Base 45%**: The business stays strong, but upside depends more on estimates catching up than on further multiple expansion; the stock likely stays event-driven and volatile.

**Bear 20%**: The multiple compresses if commercial/AI conversion slows, defense timing slips, competitive pressure rises, or hotter inflation pushes rates higher.
