## AVGO Scenario Frame

As of **2026-04-16**, **AVGO** is set up as a high-expectation AI infrastructure and software/cybersecurity compounder, but the key near-term swing factor is whether the recently highlighted **Meta AI chip partnership expansion** translates into durable revenue visibility without worsening customer concentration risk. Fundamentals support a constructive base case: margins are strong, free cash flow is large, and the gap between **77.8x TTM P/E** and **22.1x forward P/E** implies the market is underwriting a major earnings step-up. Macro is not fighting the story right now: Fed policy has been stable at **3.64%**, the **2Y-10Y curve is +0.53%**, and **VIX at 18.17** suggests manageable risk appetite.

### Bull / Base / Bear
- **Bull (35%)**: AI networking/custom silicon demand stays tight, Meta-related upside broadens to additional hyperscaler demand, and software/cybersecurity helps smooth cyclicality. Inference: AVGO can sustain premium valuation if forward EPS conversion remains credible and supply chain commentary stays favorable.
- **Base (45%)**: AI demand remains strong, but upside is partly offset by concentration concerns and already-elevated expectations. AVGO executes well enough to justify a premium, though multiple expansion is limited from here.
- **Bear (20%)**: Meta enthusiasm proves too narrow, custom-AI revenues disappoint versus embedded expectations, or macro/rates re-tighten and compress semiconductor multiples. In that setup, AVGO’s premium valuation and customer concentration become the pressure points.

### Key Signposts
- Whether management/customer commentary confirms **broadening AI customer demand**, not just Meta.
- Evidence that **TSMC strength** is flowing through to AVGO-relevant networking/custom silicon volumes.
- Any sign that software/cybersecurity is stabilizing overall mix if semiconductor expectations normalize.
- Changes in rate/inflation tone that would pressure long-duration AI beneficiaries.

### Invalidation Logic
- The **bull case** is weakened if AI upside remains concentrated in one customer or if margin/free-cash-flow conversion softens.
- The **base case** breaks if forward earnings support does not materialize quickly enough to justify the current premium.
- The **bear case** is invalidated if AVGO shows multi-customer AI design-win breadth and continued operating leverage despite elevated expectations.
