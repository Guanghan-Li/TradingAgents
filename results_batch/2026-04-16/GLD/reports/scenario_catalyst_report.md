## GLD Scenario Summary
GLD is sitting in a strong longer-term uptrend, with the 50-day average at `450.24` well above the 200-day average at `384.09`, but the near-term setup looks less clean. The macro mix is conflicted: March 2026 CPI and PPI re-accelerated, which supports gold as an inflation hedge, yet the 10-year Treasury yield at `4.29%` and a still-calm VIX at `18.17` limit urgency for a fresh safe-haven chase. Recent GLD/gold news also points to a strong rally now facing rangebound trading, lighter volume, and competition from risk assets like Bitcoin.

### Scenario Framing
- **Bull case (30%)**: GLD resumes its breakout if inflation stays sticky enough to sustain gold demand while growth/risk sentiment softens enough to revive defensive positioning or rate-cut expectations. In that case, GLD can retest and potentially clear the `509.7` 52-week high.
- **Base case (50%)**: GLD consolidates after a sharp run, with sticky inflation offset by still-elevated long yields and an orderly risk backdrop. This favors choppy trading around the 50-day area rather than an immediate trend extension.
- **Bear case (20%)**: GLD corrects if upcoming macro data keep real yields firm, the Fed stays patient, and capital rotates toward equities or Bitcoin. The technical risk in that scenario is a pullback toward the 200-day average.

### Key Signposts
- Inflation prints after the March 2026 re-acceleration: cooling favors consolidation; renewed upside can keep gold demand alive.
- Long-end yields, especially whether the 10-year Treasury yield breaks meaningfully above `4.29%`.
- Risk appetite: VIX staying subdued and continued strength in equities/crypto would pressure GLD.
- Price behavior around the 50-day average (`450.24`) and the prior 52-week high (`509.7`).

### Thesis Invalidation Logic
- The **bull** thesis weakens if GLD cannot hold trend support while real yields rise and safe-haven demand stays muted.
- The **base** thesis breaks if GLD either decisively clears the prior high on strong risk-off/flow support or loses the 200-day trend support.
- The **bear** thesis is invalidated by a clean breakout above the prior high alongside falling yields or a visible flight-to-safety bid.
