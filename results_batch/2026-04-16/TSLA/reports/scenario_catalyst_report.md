## TSLA Scenario Framing

TSLA remains a high-beta, high-duration equity where narrative execution matters as much as near-term fundamentals. The setup is asymmetric: valuation is still extremely demanding at roughly 354x TTM EPS and 140x forward EPS, while margins and cash generation remain positive but not yet strong enough to fully self-fund a $1.46T market cap on auto economics alone. The current tape is being supported by autonomy/AI5, Terafab, and Optimus optionality, but the bear case stays live if those themes fail to convert into visible earnings power.

### Scenario Summary

| Scenario | Probability | Core View | What Has To Happen |
| --- | ---: | --- | --- |
| Bull | 30% | TSLA sustains a premium multiple as investors underwrite AI/autonomy and robotics as the next earnings engine. | AI5/Optimus/Terafab progress must become more concrete, and forward EPS revisions must keep improving. |
| Base | 45% | Shares remain volatile but range-bound as narrative strength offsets still-thin auto margins and an elevated multiple. | Execution stays good enough to defend growth optionality, but not yet enough to justify another major rerating. |
| Bear | 25% | The stock derates if autonomy/robotics timelines slip or if macro/inflation keeps pressure on demand and duration-sensitive multiples. | Margin pressure, weak cash conversion, or evidence that recent demand/support was non-organic would matter quickly. |

### Key Signposts

- Bull signposts: forward EPS moves materially above current 2.77, operating margin expands from ~4.7%, and AI5/Optimus updates shift from concept to measured deployment.
- Base signposts: revenue growth holds, free cash flow stays positive, and the stock trades on optionality without a major earnings reset.
- Bear signposts: gross/operating margin compression, negative estimate revisions, weak demand quality, or macro prints that keep real-rate pressure elevated.

### Invalidation Logic

- Bull invalidates if autonomy/robotics milestones remain promotional without commercialization evidence, or if EPS revisions roll over despite strong stock performance.
- Base invalidates if TSLA either proves real monetization from AI/robotics faster than expected or, conversely, posts clear evidence of structural demand/margin erosion.
- Bear invalidates if Tesla demonstrates sustained margin recovery and converts AI5/Optimus/Terafab into credible medium-term earnings power rather than narrative optionality.
