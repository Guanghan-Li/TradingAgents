FINAL TRANSACTION PROPOSAL: **HOLD**

For `FCX`, the most relevant indicator set in this regime is `close_10_ema`, `close_50_sma`, `close_200_sma`, `macd`, `rsi`, `boll_ub`, `boll_lb`, and `atr`. This mix is appropriate because it covers short-, medium-, and long-term trend structure, momentum confirmation, overextension risk, volatility, and actionable risk management without doubling up on redundant MACD subcomponents or extra Bollinger inputs.

Technically, `FCX` is in a strong recovery-to-breakout phase. The stock fell from `68.67` on 2026-02-25 to `51.98` on 2026-03-20, then rallied back to `68.70` on 2026-04-15 before a modest pullback to `68.28` on 2026-04-16. That recovery was not random noise: price is now well above the `10 EMA` at `65.63`, above the `50 SMA` at `61.84`, and far above the `200 SMA` at `49.51`. That bullish stacking indicates the short-term swing, medium-term trend, and long-term regime are aligned higher. In a macro backdrop classified as `RISK-ON`, that alignment matters because cyclical names like `FCX` tend to perform best when both trend and regime point the same way.

Momentum confirms the move. `MACD` has improved from `-1.90` on 2026-03-30 to `+2.37` on 2026-04-16, which is a meaningful positive reversal and shows that the April advance has internal thrust, not just mechanical mean reversion. `RSI` at `66.98` is strong but not yet decisively overbought. That is constructive: it says buyers are in control, but it also warns that upside from here may be less linear than it was during the first leg off the March lows. This is not a washed-out value setup anymore; it is now a momentum continuation setup that needs either breakout confirmation or a controlled reset.

The Bollinger structure reinforces that read. On 2026-04-16, `boll_ub` sits near `72.05`, with price at `68.28`, so `FCX` is trading in the upper portion of its volatility envelope but is not yet at an extreme tag of the upper band. On 2026-04-15, the lower band was `49.08`, which highlights how far the stock has traveled from the lower end of the recent range. In practice, that means the trend is strong, but near-term reward is less attractive than it was around the `58` to `62` area. If the stock pushes cleanly through the recent high zone around `69.5`, the next likely extension is toward the low `70s`, roughly where the upper band is migrating. If it stalls, a pause back toward the `10 EMA` would be normal and not automatically bearish.

Volatility is actually improving. `ATR` has fallen from about `3.06` on 2026-04-02 to `2.55` on 2026-04-16. That decline in ATR while price trends higher is constructive because it suggests the rally is becoming more orderly rather than more chaotic. Lighter volume on the 2026-04-16 pullback (`10.13M`) versus the breakout sessions on 2026-04-08 (`20.34M`) and 2026-04-01 (`23.68M`) also argues that sellers did not show up aggressively at the first sign of hesitation. That reduces the odds that 2026-04-16 was the start of a sharp reversal.

The macro overlay is supportive, but only conditionally so. A positively sloped curve, stable labor market, and broader `RISK-ON` tape favor cyclicals and trend continuation. That supports the technical breakout case in `FCX`. The limiting factor is sticky inflation and elevated long-end yields, which can keep the dollar firm and cap valuation expansion for miners. So the chart says buyers currently control the tape, but the macro says not to overpay for that strength. That combination usually favors staying with the trend while being disciplined on entry.

Actionably, this argues for a `HOLD` rather than an outright fresh `BUY` at current levels. Existing longs can continue to ride the uptrend while `FCX` holds above the `10 EMA` and especially above the `67` to `65.5` support area. A decisive break above `69.5` with solid volume would strengthen the case for another momentum leg. For new capital, the cleaner setups are either: `1.` a confirmed breakout through `69.5`, or `2.` a pullback toward the `10 EMA` / breakout support zone that holds. A short-term risk point can be framed around roughly `1 ATR` below spot, near `65.7`, while a deeper swing invalidation sits closer to the `50 SMA` near `61.8`. If price loses the `10 EMA` and momentum rolls over, the setup degrades from bullish continuation to range repair.
