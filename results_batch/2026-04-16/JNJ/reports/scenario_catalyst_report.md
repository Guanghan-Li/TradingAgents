## Scenario Summary for JNJ

JNJ screens as a defensive compounder with low beta, strong margins, dividend support, and analyst/news momentum around a growth reacceleration narrative. The base case is that JNJ sustains premium healthcare-defensive valuation while Stelara pressure is offset by oncology, immunology, MedTech, and pipeline execution. The bull case requires visible progress toward the reported $100B revenue ambition and credible double-digit growth targets by 2030. The bear case centers on Stelara erosion, pipeline/MedTech execution misses, litigation or regulatory setbacks, and multiple compression if rates or inflation stay sticky.
