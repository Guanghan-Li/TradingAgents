## META Scenario Summary

META screens as a high-quality large-cap compounder with strong profitability (`41.3%` operating margin, `30.1%` profit margin, `30.2%` ROE), but the setup is no longer cheap on trailing numbers (`28.8x` TTM P/E) and depends on the market accepting the step-up implied by `19.0x` forward P/E. The near-term readthrough is mixed: company-specific news is modestly positive on AI infrastructure and product iteration, while Quest price hikes point to hardware cost pressure and possible Reality Labs demand friction.

My base case for META is that core advertising remains resilient, AI spend stays strategically justified, and Threads/engagement initiatives add optionality without changing the main earnings story. The bull case is a cleaner AI monetization and ad-efficiency acceleration that lets investors lean into the forward EPS ramp. The bear case is that cost inflation, headset demand friction, and heavy AI capex arrive before monetization, compressing sentiment and the multiple.
