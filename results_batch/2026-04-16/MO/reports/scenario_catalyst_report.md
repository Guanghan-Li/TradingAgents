Bull case requires Q1 commentary to confirm stable combustibles pricing, durable free cash flow, and reduced-risk optionality. Bear case centers on earnings disappointment, regulatory pressure, or a rates/inflation backdrop that makes a 6.58% dividend yield less compelling versus Treasuries.
