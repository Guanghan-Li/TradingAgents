The macro backdrop is good enough to support `MO` as a hold, but not attractive enough to justify aggressive multiple expansion.

For traders, the key nuance is that `MO` is not oversold enough to force entry and not strong enough to justify breakout buying. That is a classic hold/no-chase condition. If you are income-oriented, the chart is still acceptable. If you are momentum-oriented, patience is the higher-quality decision until either trend re-acceleration or a more attractive pullback develops.
