## SCCO Scenario And Catalyst Map

Southern Copper Corporation (`SCCO`) is a high-quality copper equity with very strong profitability, but the setup is valuation-sensitive. Current fundamentals show a premium multiple profile: market cap about $155.5B, TTM P/E 36.3x, forward P/E 30.5x, P/B 14.1x, profit margin 32.3%, operating margin 54.5%, ROE 42.7%, and FCF about $3.34B. The stock is also extended versus its 200-day average, with the 50-day average near $188 and 200-day average near $141, after recent headlines noting a 136% one-year surge.

Base case is a constructive but disciplined consolidation scenario: copper fundamentals and SCCO margins remain strong, but valuation already prices in a large amount of growth and scarcity value. Bull case requires sustained copper strength, higher forward EPS confidence, and continued analyst upgrades. Bear case is mainly multiple compression if copper prices, China/industrial demand, or risk appetite weaken.
