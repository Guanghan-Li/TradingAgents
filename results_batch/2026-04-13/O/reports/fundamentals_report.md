# Fundamental Analysis Report: Realty Income Corporation (O)
**Report Date:** April 13, 2026  
**Sector:** Real Estate | **Industry:** REIT - Retail

## Executive Summary

Realty Income Corporation is a retail-focused REIT trading at $63.30 with a market cap of $59.0B. The company demonstrates stable operational performance with a 5.07% dividend yield, though valuation metrics suggest premium pricing relative to historical norms. Recent quarterly results show revenue growth momentum but margin compression from elevated interest costs and asset impairments.

## Company Profile & Business Model

Realty Income operates as a monthly dividend-paying REIT specializing in retail properties under long-term net lease agreements. The company's portfolio consists primarily of single-tenant commercial properties leased to investment-grade tenants, providing predictable cash flows. With $53.4B in investment properties as of Q4 2025, the company maintains a diversified portfolio across retail sectors.

**FINAL TRANSACTION PROPOSAL: HOLD**

**Rationale:** While Realty Income demonstrates strong operational cash flow ($1.2B quarterly) and maintains an attractive 5.07% dividend yield, the current valuation at 54x trailing PE offers insufficient margin of safety. Existing holders should maintain positions for income, but new capital deployment should wait for either: (1) price pullback to $57-60 range, (2) evidence of impairment cycle ending, or (3) margin stabilization in upcoming Q1 2026 results. The 10Y Treasury at 4.29% also pressures REIT valuations, limiting near-term upside.
