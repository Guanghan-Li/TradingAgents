**Risk Appetite**: The fact that chip stocks are hitting new highs and leading rallies suggests strong risk appetite in the market. Investors are rotating into growth and technology names, which is favorable for high-growth infrastructure plays.

**Sector Rotation**: The strength in semiconductors and infrastructure stocks suggests institutional money is flowing into the "picks and shovels" of the AI revolution rather than just pure-play AI software companies.
