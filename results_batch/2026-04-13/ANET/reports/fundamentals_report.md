## Executive Summary

Arista Networks (ANET) demonstrates exceptional fundamental strength with accelerating revenue growth, expanding margins, and robust cash generation. The company's Q4 2025 results show revenue of $2.49B (+28.9% YoY), marking the strongest quarterly performance in the dataset. With a fortress balance sheet ($10.7B in cash/investments), zero debt, and 39% profit margins, ANET exhibits premium quality characteristics that justify its elevated valuation multiples.

## Financial Performance Analysis

### Revenue Trajectory (Quarterly Sequential Growth)
- Q4 2025: $2,488M (+7.8% QoQ, +28.9% YoY)
- Q3 2025: $2,308M (+4.7% QoQ)
- Q2 2025: $2,205M (+10.0% QoQ)
- Q1 2025: $2,005M (+3.9% QoQ)
- Q4 2024: $1,930M (baseline)

**Key Insight:** Revenue growth is accelerating, with Q4 2025 showing the strongest absolute dollar gain ($180M QoQ increase). The company is on a $10B+ annual run rate, significantly ahead of TTM revenue of $9.0B.

### Profitability Metrics (Q4 2025)
- **Gross Margin:** 62.9% (up from 63.8% in Q4 2024)
- **Operating Margin:** 41.5% (vs 41.4% Q4 2024) - remarkably stable
- **Net Margin:** 38.4% (vs 41.5% Q4 2024)
- **EBITDA Margin:** 42.4%

**Key Insight:** Margins remain best-in-class despite scale expansion. The slight compression in net margin is primarily due to higher tax rates (16.0% in Q4 2025 vs 9.9% in Q4 2024), not operational deterioration.

### Earnings Power
- **Q4 2025 EPS:** $0.75 diluted (+21% YoY)
- **TTM EPS:** $2.76
- **Forward EPS:** $4.27 (implies 55% growth)
- **ROE:** 31.4%
- **ROA:** 14.4%

**Key Insight:** The forward PE of 35.4x appears reasonable given the implied 55% EPS growth trajectory. Current valuation reflects strong execution and AI/cloud infrastructure tailwinds.

- **Tangible Book Value:** $12.0B
- **Price-to-Book:** 15.4x

**Key Insight:** The balance sheet is fortress-grade. The company is self-funding growth with no financial leverage risk. Rising inventory could signal either demand strength (building for orders) or potential softness (excess stock).

### Deferred Revenue (Critical Metric)
- **Current Deferred Revenue:** $4.0B (Q4 2025) vs $1.7B (Q4 2024) - **+132% YoY**
- **Non-Current Deferred Revenue:** $1.4B vs $1.1B (+29% YoY)
- **Total Deferred Revenue:** $5.4B (+98% YoY)

**Key Insight:** This is the most bullish data point. Deferred revenue represents contracted future sales. The explosive 132% growth in current deferred revenue provides exceptional revenue visibility for 2026 and signals accelerating customer commitments, likely driven by AI infrastructure buildouts.
