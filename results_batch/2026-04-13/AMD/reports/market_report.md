## Momentum Indicators

### RSI (70.21) - Overbought Warning
The RSI has crossed above 70, entering overbought territory for the first time since the rally began. This does **not** mean an immediate reversal, but it does signal:
- **Reduced upside potential** in the very short term
- **Increased probability** of a pullback or consolidation
- **Need for caution** on new long entries at current levels

**Historical context:** In strong trends, RSI can remain elevated (70-80) for extended periods. However, the first touch of 70 often precedes a 3-7% pullback before the trend resumes.

### MACD (8.85) - Strong Bullish Momentum
The MACD line has surged from negative territory (-1.33 on March 30) to +8.85, one of the steepest momentum accelerations in recent months. Key observations:
- **MACD crossed above the signal line** around April 1, generating a buy signal
- **Histogram expanding rapidly**, confirming momentum strength
- **No bearish divergence** visible; price highs are confirmed by momentum highs

**Interpretation:** Momentum is robust and accelerating. The MACD supports continuation, but the steep angle suggests the move may be overextended in the short term.

## Volatility & Risk Assessment

### Bollinger Bands - Testing Upper Boundary
- **Upper Band (245.02):** Price is touching/exceeding the upper band, a classic sign of overbought conditions or a breakout attempt
- **Lower Band (181.05):** Provides a wide safety net, reflecting recent volatility
- **Band Width:** The bands are wide (64 points), indicating elevated volatility

**Interpretation:** When price rides the upper Bollinger Band in a strong trend, it can signal either:
1. **Breakout continuation** (bullish) - price establishes a new range above the band
2. **Mean reversion setup** (bearish) - price snaps back toward the middle band (~213)

Given the RSI overbought reading, scenario 2 (mean reversion) is more likely in the next 3-5 days.

### ATR (9.80) - Moderate Volatility
The Average True Range has declined from ~10.5 in early April to 9.80, suggesting volatility is normalizing after the breakout. This is healthy for trend continuation but also means:
- **Stop-loss placement:** Use 1.5x ATR (~$15) below entry for swing trades
- **Position sizing:** Adjust for $10-15 daily swings
