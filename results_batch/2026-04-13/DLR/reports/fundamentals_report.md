# Fundamental Analysis Report: Digital Realty Trust, Inc. (DLR)
**Report Date:** April 13, 2026  
**Analyst Role:** Fundamental Researcher

## Executive Summary

Digital Realty Trust (DLR) is a leading data center REIT demonstrating strong operational momentum with significant outperformance versus sector peers. The company has delivered +23.95% YTD returns, substantially outpacing the Real Estate sector ETF (XLRE) by +17.21%. Recent quarterly results show robust revenue growth, improving margins, and strategic capital deployment, though elevated leverage and a premium valuation warrant careful monitoring.

## Company Profile

**Sector:** Real Estate  
**Industry:** REIT - Specialty (Data Centers)  
**Market Capitalization:** $66.76 billion  
**Current Valuation Metrics:**
- P/E Ratio (TTM): 53.16x
- Forward P/E: 58.97x
- Price-to-Book: 2.95x
- Dividend Yield: 2.58%

DLR operates as a specialized REIT focused on data center infrastructure, providing colocation and interconnection services to enterprise and hyperscale customers globally.
