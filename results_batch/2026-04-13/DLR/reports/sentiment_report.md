## Actionable Insights for Traders & Investors

### Bullish Factors (Strong)
- **Analyst momentum:** Three upgrades in 10 days with 15-20% upside to consensus targets
- **Strategic positioning:** Leading AI infrastructure play in high-growth Asia market
- **Scale advantage:** S$7B commitment demonstrates financial strength and market confidence

The analyst community has responded with clear bullish signals, and the strategic logic is compelling. **Overall Stance:** Cautiously bullish with preference for entry on weakness rather than chasing current levels.
