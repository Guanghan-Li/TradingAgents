## Macro-Technical Synthesis

### Macro Backdrop: Constructive but Not Euphoric

The **risk-on regime** (VIX falling, cyclicals leading, S&P above 200-day MA) is supportive for growth stocks like PLTR. However, two macro risks loom:

1. **PPI Inflation Spike (+2.08% MoM):** If this feeds into CPI, the Fed may pivot hawkish, compressing tech multiples. PLTR's long-duration profile makes it vulnerable to rising discount rates.
2. **30-Year Yield at 4.90%:** Elevated long-end yields pressure high-valuation growth stocks. A further steepening (e.g., to 5.10%) would be a headwind.

**Net Assessment:** The macro environment is **neutral-to-positive** for PLTR, but not strong enough to override the bearish technical setup. A Fed pivot to cuts (if inflation cools) would be a major catalyst, but that's not the base case.
