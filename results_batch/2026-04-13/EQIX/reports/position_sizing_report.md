# Position Sizing Analysis: EQIX (Equinix, Inc.)

## Executive Summary
Equinix is a high-quality data center REIT with strong fundamentals and momentum, currently trading at 52-week highs. The business model is compelling (essential digital infrastructure), profitability is solid (14.6% margin, $2.8B FCF), and the chart shows a clean 15% rally over two months. However, the elevated valuation (PE 76) and technical position at all-time highs warrant a disciplined, scaled entry.

## Sizing Recommendation
- **Target Position:** 3.5% of portfolio
- **Initial Entry:** 1.75% (50% starter)
- **Max Portfolio Loss:** 1.5%
- **Stop Loss:** ~$1,010 (2× ATR = $43 below current price of $1,053)

## Rationale
**Conviction: MODERATE-HIGH**

The thesis rests on three pillars: (1) Equinix operates mission-critical infrastructure with pricing power and recurring revenue; (2) the 15% two-month rally reflects genuine strength, not speculation; (3) current ATR of $21.38 (~2% of price) allows for a reasonable stop without getting shaken out by noise.

**Why not larger?** Valuation (76× trailing earnings) and the fact we're buying the 52-week high. A 1.75% starter limits regret if we see a 5-10% pullback, while leaving room to add on a successful retest of $1,000-$1,020 support.

**Risk Management:** A 2× ATR stop at $1,010 represents a ~4% price decline. On a 3.5% full position, that's a 1.4% portfolio hit—acceptable for a high-conviction REIT with structural tailwinds. Scale the second half only after confirming support or on a breakout above $1,060 with volume.
