# Dell Technologies Inc. (DELL) - Fundamental Analysis Report
**Report Date:** April 13, 2026

## Executive Summary

Dell Technologies has demonstrated exceptional operational momentum in recent quarters, with strong revenue growth, margin expansion, and robust cash generation. The stock has significantly outperformed both sector peers and the Technology ETF benchmark, driven by AI infrastructure demand and operational efficiency improvements.

## Financial Performance Analysis

### Revenue Trajectory (Quarterly)
Dell's revenue shows strong sequential growth with notable seasonality:
- **Q4 FY2026 (Jan 31):** $33.38B (+23.6% QoQ)
- **Q3 FY2026 (Oct 31):** $27.01B (-9.3% QoQ)
- **Q2 FY2026 (Jul 31):** $29.78B (+27.4% QoQ)
- **TTM Revenue:** $113.54B

The Q4 surge to $33.38B represents the strongest quarter in the dataset, indicating robust demand particularly in enterprise infrastructure and AI-related hardware.

**Operating Margin:** 9.62% (TTM) with Q4 operating income of $3.15B

**EPS Trajectory:**

### Operating Efficiency

**EBITDA:** $11.46B TTM with Q4 at $3.98B, representing strong cash generation capability.

**R&D Investment:** Consistent ~$750-800M per quarter ($797M in Q4), showing commitment to innovation without over-spending.

**SG&A Control:** $2.79B in Q4 (8.4% of revenue), demonstrating operational leverage as revenue scales.
