Trough margins are more likely 20-25%, implying breakeven to low-single-digit EPS.

That said, the balance sheet materially limits downside. Near net cash ($13.9B cash vs $10.8B debt), 2.9x current ratio, and $11.9B quarterly operating cash flow mean Micron survives downturns without covenant stress. This isn't a leveraged cyclical that implodes - it's a cash machine with temporary margin pressure.
