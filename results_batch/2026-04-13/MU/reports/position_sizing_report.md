# Position Sizing Analysis: MU (Micron Technology)

## Market Context
MU has experienced extreme volatility over the past 60 days. After reaching an all-time high of $471.34 on March 18, the stock suffered a sharp -32% correction to $321.80 by March 30, likely driven by broader semiconductor sector concerns or profit-taking. The stock has since recovered ~30% to $417.54 as of April 13, suggesting institutional support at lower levels.

## Fundamental Anchor
The thesis quality is **strong**:
- Forward PE of 4.25 vs. TTM PE of 19.7 signals explosive earnings growth expectations (Forward EPS: $98.16 vs. TTM EPS: $21.20)
- Profit margin of 41.5% and ROE of 39.8% demonstrate operational excellence
- Market cap $471B with $24.1B net income (TTM) and $2.9B free cash flow
- Debt-to-equity of 14.9 is elevated but manageable given strong cash generation
- Current ratio of 2.9 provides liquidity cushion

## Volatility & Risk Assessment
- **ATR (14-day)**: ~$25.20 (6.0% of current price)
- **Recent range**: $321–$471 (47% peak-to-trough swing)
- **Beta**: 1.606 (60% more volatile than market)

The elevated ATR and recent whipsaw action demand **defensive sizing** despite strong fundamentals. A 1.5x ATR stop (~$38 or 9% below entry) balances noise tolerance with capital preservation.
