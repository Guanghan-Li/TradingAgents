## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Meta Platforms delivered robust Q4 2025 results with revenue of $59.9B (+24% YoY) and operating margins of 41.3%, demonstrating strong core business momentum. However, the company is in a significant investment cycle, with capital expenditures reaching $69.7B annually as it builds AI infrastructure. The stock has underperformed peers (-14% over 6 months) despite solid fundamentals, creating a potential value opportunity for investors with a long-term horizon willing to look through near-term CapEx concerns.
