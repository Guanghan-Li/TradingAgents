# Amazon (AMZN) Scenario & Catalyst Analysis
**Analysis Date:** 2026-04-13

## Executive Summary

Amazon trades at **$238.66** (implied from market cap), ~8% below its 52-week high of $258.60, with a forward P/E of 25.4× reflecting market confidence in AI-driven margin expansion. The company's $200B AI infrastructure commitment and 10.8% profit margin (up from historical 5-7%) position it at an inflection point: either AWS/AI monetization accelerates margin leverage (bull), or capex intensity compresses near-term FCF without commensurate revenue growth (bear).

---

## Scenario Framework

### **Bull Case (40% probability)**  
**Thesis:** AWS AI services capture 30%+ of enterprise GenAI spend by 2027; retail margin expansion continues via automation; advertising grows 20%+ annually.  
**Valuation Implication:** 12-month PT $290–310 (30–35× forward EPS of ~$9.50).  
**Key Signposts:**  
- Q2 2026 AWS revenue growth reaccelerates to 18%+ YoY (vs. ~15% recent trend)  
- Operating margin reaches 11.5%+ by year-end (currently 10.5%)  
- Anthropic partnership yields measurable AWS customer wins (5+ Fortune 100 logos)  

### **Base Case (45% probability)**  
**Thesis:** AI capex pays off gradually; AWS grows 14–16%; retail remains stable; margin hovers 10–11%.  
**Valuation Implication:** 12-month PT $240–265 (26–28× forward EPS).  
**Key Signposts:**  
- AWS growth steady at 14–16% with modest AI contribution  
- FCF remains $20–25B annually despite elevated capex  
- Retail same-store sales grow 6–8% (in line with e-commerce TAM)  

### **Bear Case (15% probability)**  
**Thesis:** AI capex ($200B over 5 years = $40B/year) crowds out FCF; AWS loses share to Microsoft/Google; regulatory headwinds intensify.  
**Valuation Implication:** 12-month PT $180–210 (20–23× forward EPS).  
**Key Signposts:**  
- AWS growth decelerates below 12% for two consecutive quarters  
- FCF turns negative or sub-$10B annually  
- FTC/EU antitrust actions force structural divestitures (e.g., Prime Video, MGM)

## Macro Overlay

- **Fed Policy:** Rates stable at 3.64%; 2Y-10Y spread +51bps suggests no imminent recession. Neutral for AMZN.  
- **Inflation:** CPI +0.87% MoM (Mar 2026) and PPI +2.08% (Feb) indicate sticky input costs but manageable for a platform with pricing power.  
- **Consumer Health:** Unemployment 4.3% (low); nonfarm payrolls +178K. Supports retail demand.  
- **VIX 19.23:** Moderate volatility; no panic, but not complacent. Allows for measured position-building.

1. **Bull case hinges on Q1 AWS acceleration** — if growth prints >17%, probability shifts to 55%.

3. **Bear case requires multiple failures** (AWS deceleration + margin compression + regulatory shock) — low probability but high impact.
