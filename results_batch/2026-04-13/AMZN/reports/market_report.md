# AMZN Technical Analysis Report
**Date: 2026-04-13**

## Executive Summary

Amazon has staged a powerful breakout rally, surging from $213.77 on April 7 to $238.76 by April 13—a gain of nearly 12% in just four trading sessions. The move is technically valid, supported by bullish MACD crossover, rising moving averages, and strong volume. However, short-term momentum indicators (RSI at 71.46, price above upper Bollinger Band) signal overbought conditions. The macro backdrop remains constructive (RISK-ON regime, Fed on hold, solid growth), supporting continuation but warranting caution on immediate entry.

**Price Action:**

- The rally extended through April 9-10, with price reaching $240.43 intraday before settling at $238.76.
- Volume spiked to 65.96M on April 9 (well above the 30-50M average), confirming conviction behind the move.

**Interpretation:** The trend has decisively shifted from consolidation/weakness (late March lows near $199) to a strong uptrend. The breakout above the March 5 high of $220.47 was the technical trigger, and follow-through has been robust.
