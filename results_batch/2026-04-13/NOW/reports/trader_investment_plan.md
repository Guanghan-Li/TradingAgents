Where's that coming from when revenue is growing mid-teens and operating margins are compressing? The "cheap" valuation is a trap—it's pricing in a miracle that the current environment won't deliver.

Capital is flowing to semiconductors (Marvell, Broadcom hitting records) and away from SaaS. NOW is down 52% while its sector peers show mixed but better performance. This isn't noise—it's a structural shift in where growth capital is being deployed.
