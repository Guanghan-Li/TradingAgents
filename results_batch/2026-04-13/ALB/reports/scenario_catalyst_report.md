# Scenario & Catalyst Analysis: Albemarle Corporation (ALB)
**Analysis Date:** 2026-04-13

## Executive Summary

Albemarle is emerging from a lithium-market trough with strong technical momentum (+203% YoY, +22% YTD) but weak fundamentals (TTM EPS -$5.76, net loss -$677M). The bull case hinges on lithium demand recovery and strategic positioning in the US critical minerals consortium; the bear case centers on persistent oversupply, high leverage (D/E 33.95), and execution risk. Upcoming earnings and geopolitical developments (Hormuz Strait tensions) are near-term catalysts.

## Scenario Map

| Scenario | Probability | Thesis | Valuation Implication |
|----------|-------------|--------|----------------------|
| **Bull** | 35% | Lithium demand inflects positively driven by EV adoption acceleration and supply discipline; ALB executes cost cuts and benefits from US critical minerals consortium positioning; forward EPS of $8.64 materializes. | Forward P/E 21.5× justified; upside to $230+ (UBS/Oppenheimer targets). |
| **Base** | 45% | Lithium prices stabilize at current levels; ALB returns to modest profitability by H2 2026; leverage remains elevated but manageable; sector consolidation provides modest pricing power. | Fair value $180–$200; multiple compression risk if recovery delays. |
| **Bear** | 20% | Lithium oversupply persists through 2027; Chinese capacity additions overwhelm demand growth; ALB's high leverage forces asset sales or equity dilution; geopolitical disruptions (Hormuz) impact supply chains. | Downside to $120–$140; covenant risk and dividend cut likely. |

---

## Key Signposts by Scenario

### Bull Case Signposts
- Q1 2026 earnings beat with positive forward guidance (EPS >$2.00/quarter)
- Lithium carbonate spot prices stabilize above $15k/ton
- US critical minerals consortium announces first contracts
- China EV sales growth reaccelerates (>30% YoY)

### Base Case Signposts
- Earnings meet lowered expectations; guidance remains cautious
- Lithium prices range-bound $12–15k/ton
- Debt-to-equity ratio begins declining (target <30 by YE 2026)
- Market share stable in key geographies

### Bear Case Signposts
- Earnings miss; guidance cut further
- Lithium prices fall below $10k/ton
- Covenant breach warnings or credit rating downgrade
- Major customer (Tesla, GM) announces supply diversification away from ALB
