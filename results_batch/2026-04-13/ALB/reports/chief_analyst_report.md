The technical setup? Sure, it looks good. Bull flag breakout, golden cross, all that. But technicals are lagging indicators. They tell you where sentiment is, not where earnings are going. And right now, sentiment is euphoric. When a stock is up 200%+ and everyone is bullish, that's not when you buy—that's when you fade.

Here's my bottom line: the bear case doesn't require catastrophe. It just requires reality to fall short of the heroic assumptions already priced in. If forward EPS comes in at $5-6 instead of $8.64—still a recovery, just not a miracle—and the multiple compresses to 15x (normal for a cyclical), you're looking at fair value of $75-90. That's 50-60% downside from here.
