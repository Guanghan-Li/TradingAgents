# VICI Properties (VICI) – Scenario & Catalyst Analysis
**Analysis Date:** 2026-04-13

## Executive Summary

VICI Properties is a gaming/hospitality REIT trading at **$28.16** with a **6.34% dividend yield** and a forward P/E of **9.54x**—attractive relative to historical REIT valuations. The company just announced its **8th consecutive annual dividend increase**, reinforcing its income-focused thesis. However, Wolfe Research recently cut its price target from $40 to $33 (still Outperform), signaling valuation compression amid higher-for-longer rate expectations.

**Macro backdrop:** Fed funds stable at 3.64%, 10Y Treasury at 4.29%, and a positively sloped yield curve suggest no imminent rate cuts but also no further tightening. CPI remains elevated (+0.87% MoM), keeping pressure on rate-sensitive REITs.

## Recommendation Framework

- **Bull case activation:** Requires Fed pivot (2+ cuts priced by Q3) + gaming strength + occupancy > 99%. **Upside to $35+**.
- **Base case (most likely):** Hold for income; dividend yield of 6.34% compensates for limited capital appreciation. **Range-bound $28–$32**.
- **Bear case trigger:** 10Y > 4.75% sustained + tenant stress + dividend cut. **Downside to $24**.

**Current positioning:** VICI is priced for the **base case**. The 8th dividend increase and low forward P/E offer a margin of safety, but rate sensitivity and tenant concentration remain key risks. Monitor Q1 earnings (late April) and May FOMC closely.
