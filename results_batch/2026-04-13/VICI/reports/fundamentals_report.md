# VICI Properties Inc. (VICI) - Fundamental Analysis Report
**Report Date: April 13, 2026**

## Executive Summary

VICI Properties Inc. is a diversified REIT with a market capitalization of $30.1B, trading at a P/E of 10.78x with a compelling 6.34% dividend yield. The company demonstrates strong profitability metrics (69.3% profit margin, 80.3% operating margin) but faces headwinds in relative performance, underperforming its Real Estate sector peers across all timeframes.

## Financial Performance Analysis

### Profitability & Margins
VICI exhibits exceptional profitability for a REIT:
- **Profit Margin**: 69.28% - significantly above typical REIT standards
- **Operating Margin**: 80.31% - indicates highly efficient operations
- **Return on Equity**: 10.22% - moderate but stable for the REIT sector
- **Return on Assets**: 4.95% - reflects asset-heavy business model

### Revenue & Earnings Trends (Quarterly)
Recent quarterly revenue shows stability with modest growth:
- Q4 2025: $1,013.1M (+3.8% vs Q4 2024)
- Q3 2025: $1,007.5M (+3.3% vs Q3 2024)
- Q2 2025: $1,001.3M
- Q1 2025: $984.2M

**Net Income trajectory** (quarterly):

The Q2 2025 spike in net income ($865M) followed by normalization suggests potential one-time gains or seasonal factors. The company maintains consistent profitability despite quarter-to-quarter volatility.

### Balance Sheet Strength

**Assets & Equity**:
- Total Assets: $46.7B (Q4 2025)
- Stockholders' Equity: $27.8B
- Investment Properties: $23.9B (core asset base)

**Debt Profile**:
- Total Debt: $17.7B
- Net Debt: $16.2B
- Debt-to-Equity: 62.68 - elevated but typical for REITs
- Current Ratio: 17.23 - exceptionally strong liquidity

The high current ratio (17.23) is unusual and driven by $18.7B in receivables, suggesting significant lease or financing arrangements with tenants.

### Cash Flow Analysis

**Operating Cash Flow** remains robust:
- Q4 2025: $691.9M
- Q3 2025: $586.3M
- Q2 2025: $639.9M
- Q1 2025: $591.9M

**Free Cash Flow** consistently strong:
- Q4 2025: $691.6M
- Q3 2025: $586.1M
- TTM Free Cash Flow: $1.47B

**Capital Allocation**:
- Dividends paid: ~$480M per quarter ($1.92B annually)
- Dividend payout ratio: ~78% of operating cash flow
- Minimal capex ($302K-$1.1M per quarter) - typical for triple-net lease REITs

The company returned capital through debt refinancing in Q2 2025 ($1.46B issuance, $1.41B repayment), optimizing its capital structure.

## Valuation Metrics

- **P/E Ratio**: 10.78x (TTM) vs Forward P/E of 9.54x - suggests modest growth expectations
- **Price-to-Book**: 1.08x - trading near book value
- **EPS**: $2.61 (TTM), Forward EPS: $2.95 (+13% expected growth)
- **Dividend Yield**: 6.34% - highly attractive in current rate environment

**Valuation Assessment**: VICI trades at a discount to historical REIT multiples, offering value for income-focused investors. The forward P/E compression suggests the market anticipates earnings growth.

## Risk Factors

1. **Tenant Concentration**: Gaming/hospitality exposure creates cyclical risk
2. **Relative Underperformance**: Persistent lag vs. peers suggests structural concerns
3. **High Debt Load**: 62.7x debt-to-equity requires careful monitoring in rising rate scenarios
4. **Receivables Concentration**: $18.7B receivables (40% of assets) creates credit risk if tenants face distress

## Investment Thesis

**Bull Case**:
- Exceptional dividend yield (6.34%) with sustainable payout ratio
- Strong free cash flow generation ($1.47B TTM)
- Attractive valuation (10.78x P/E, 1.08x P/B)
- Stable macro environment with Fed on hold

**Bear Case**:
- Severe underperformance vs. sector (-12.12% 6-month alpha)

- Limited growth trajectory (ranked last among peers)
