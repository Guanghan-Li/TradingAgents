NVIDIA demonstrates exceptional financial performance with record-breaking profitability, though recent stock performance lags peers. ## Conclusion

NVIDIA's fundamentals remain exceptional with record profitability, fortress balance sheet, and sustained revenue growth. The company's $34.9B quarterly free cash flow and minimal debt provide substantial downside protection, while the 2.34 beta suggests volatility will persist.

**For traders: The disconnect between fundamental strength and relative price weakness may present opportunity, though near-term momentum favors AMD and AVGO. Watch for inventory normalization and sustained sequential revenue growth as catalysts.**
