NVIDIA operates primarily through **Data Center** (dominant, ~80%+ of revenue), **Gaming**, **Professional Visualization**, and **Automotive/Embedded** segments. The explosive sequential revenue growth (Q4 FY26: $68.1B vs Q3: $57B vs Q2: $46.7B) signals Data Center AI accelerator demand remains the primary driver.

- **Concentration risk**: Data Center likely represents 75-85% of revenue, creating single-segment dependency
