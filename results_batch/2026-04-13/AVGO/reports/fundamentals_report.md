# Broadcom Inc. (AVGO) Fundamental Analysis Report
**Report Date:** April 13, 2026

## Executive Summary

Broadcom demonstrates exceptional momentum with **+19.87% weekly performance** and strong fundamental positioning. The company trades at a premium valuation (PE 73.47) but shows robust profitability metrics with 36.6% net margin and 33.4% ROE. Recent quarterly results reveal accelerating revenue growth driven by AI infrastructure demand, though high debt levels (debt-to-equity 82.7) warrant monitoring.

## Company Profile

**Sector:** Technology | **Industry:** Semiconductors

Broadcom operates as a diversified semiconductor and infrastructure software provider, with significant exposure to AI/ML infrastructure, data center networking, and enterprise software following the VMware acquisition.

## Financial Performance Analysis

### Revenue Trajectory & Growth Acceleration

Q1 FY2026 (Jan 31, 2026) revenue reached **$19.31B**, representing:
- **+7.2% sequential growth** from Q4 FY2025 ($18.02B)
- **+29.5% year-over-year growth** from Q1 FY2025 ($14.92B)
- Sustained acceleration: Q4→Q1 growth rate exceeded Q3→Q4 (+7.2% vs +4.8%)

The revenue trajectory shows consistent quarterly gains: $14.92B → $15.00B → $15.95B → $18.02B → $19.31B, indicating strong demand momentum particularly in AI-related semiconductor products.

**Operating Margin:** 44.9% (TTM)
- Operating income of $8.67B on $19.31B revenue (Q1 FY2026)
- Demonstrates operational leverage as revenue scales

**Net Margin:** 36.6% (TTM)
- Q1 FY2026 net income: $7.35B (38.1% margin)
- Significantly above semiconductor industry average (~20-25%)

### Earnings Quality & Cash Generation

- Down from Q4 FY2025 ($1.74) due to tax timing, but up from Q1 FY2025 ($1.14)
- **+31.6% year-over-year EPS growth**

**Free Cash Flow:** $8.01B (Q1 FY2026)

**Operating Cash Flow:** $8.26B with minimal capex ($250M), yielding exceptional FCF conversion of 97%.
