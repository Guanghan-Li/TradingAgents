## Macro-Technical Integration

The macro report highlights **late-cycle normalization with sticky inflation** and a **Fed on hold**. The PPI surge (+2.08% MoM) is the key risk, as it removes the tailwind of Fed easing and keeps discount rates elevated. For GOOGL, this means:

1. **Valuation pressure:** Higher 10Y yields (4.29%) pressure growth stock multiples. GOOGL's rally is impressive, but it is fighting a macro headwind.
2. **Earnings-driven market:** In this regime, GOOGL must deliver strong earnings and AI monetization to justify further upside. The technical rally is ahead of fundamentals—watch for earnings catalysts.
3. **Risk-on regime supports momentum:** The RISK-ON classification (+3/6 score) supports cyclical and growth stocks, which is a tailwind for GOOGL. However, the "medium confidence" rating suggests this regime is fragile.

**Actionable Insight:** The technical setup is bullish, but the macro backdrop is neutral-to-negative. This creates a **tactical long opportunity** rather than a strategic buy-and-hold. Traders should take profits into strength and avoid chasing above $328. If the April/May CPI/PPI prints show re-acceleration, expect a sharp reversal.
