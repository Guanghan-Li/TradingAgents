## Risk Factors Identified

### 1. Elevated Expectations Risk
The article "5 Stocks That Can Break Your Heart This Week" specifically warns that TSM is among stocks with high expectations. If results merely meet (rather than beat) estimates, the stock could face selling pressure despite solid fundamentals.

### 2. Geopolitical Sensitivity
While not directly mentioned in TSM-specific articles, the Nvidia/Hormuz blockade news reminds investors that semiconductor supply chains remain vulnerable to geopolitical disruptions. TSM's Taiwan location remains a structural risk factor.

### 3. Sector-Wide Earnings Test
The pairing of TSM with ASML in earnings coverage suggests the market views this as a test for the entire semiconductor capital equipment and manufacturing ecosystem. Weak results could trigger sector-wide selling.

### For Short-Term Traders (Earnings Play):

**Bullish Case:**
- Strong pre-earnings momentum and analyst confidence

- Sector tailwinds provide supportive backdrop
