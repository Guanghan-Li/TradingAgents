## Segment Analysis: Taiwan Semiconductor Manufacturing (TSM)

### Business Unit Overview

TSM operates as a pure-play semiconductor foundry with revenue concentration in **advanced process nodes** driven by AI/HPC demand. The company's Q4 2025 revenue reached NT$1,046B (up 20.5% YoY), with sequential acceleration throughout 2025 indicating robust demand across key segments.

**Key Segment Dynamics:**

**Advanced Nodes (3nm/5nm/7nm)** - Estimated 60-65% of revenue
- Primary growth driver, benefiting from AI chip demand (Nvidia, AMD, Apple)
- Gross margins exceeding 60%, supported by leading-edge pricing power
- News indicates Q1 2026 earnings likely to beat on continued AI boom
- 3nm ramp accelerating; 2nm production planned for 2025-2026

**Mature Nodes (16nm+)** - Estimated 25-30% of revenue  
- Automotive, IoT, industrial applications
- More stable but slower growth; cyclical exposure to auto demand
- Lower margins but high utilization rates

**Specialty Technologies** - Estimated 5-10% of revenue
- RF, embedded memory, sensors, power management
- Niche positioning with moderate growth

### Margin Trajectory

Operating margins expanded to **54.0%** in Q4 2025 (vs 49.0% in Q4 2024), driven by:
- Advanced node mix shift (higher-margin 3nm/5nm revenue growing faster)
- Pricing discipline amid capacity constraints
- Operating leverage as R&D intensity (6.2% of revenue) scales with volume

Gross margin at **62.3%** reflects premium positioning and technology leadership. Sequential margin improvement throughout 2025 (Q1: 58.8% → Q4: 62.3%) signals favorable product mix evolution.

### Concentration Risks

- **Customer concentration**: Top 10 customers likely represent 70%+ of revenue; Nvidia/Apple/AMD exposure creates single-customer risk
- **Geographic concentration**: 100% manufacturing in Taiwan creates geopolitical risk (China tensions, Strait of Hormuz supply chain concerns per recent news)
- **Technology concentration**: Advanced node revenue highly dependent on AI/HPC cycle sustainability

### Value Drivers & Trading Implications

| Segment | Revenue Share | Growth Outlook | Margin Trend | Trading Implication |
|---------|---------------|----------------|--------------|---------------------|
| Advanced Nodes (3nm/5nm/7nm) | 60-65% | Strong (AI-driven) | Expanding | **BUY** - AI tailwind intact, pricing power |
| Mature Nodes (16nm+) | 25-30% | Moderate | Stable | **HOLD** - Cyclical headwinds offset by utilization |
| Specialty Tech | 5-10% | Stable | Stable | **NEUTRAL** - Limited impact on overall thesis |

**Earnings Catalyst**: Q1 2026 results (expected mid-April) likely to beat consensus on AI demand strength, per multiple news sources. Forward P/E of 20.1x appears reasonable given 20%+ revenue growth and margin expansion trajectory.

**Key Risks**: Geopolitical tensions (Taiwan), customer concentration, potential AI demand normalization in 2H 2026.
