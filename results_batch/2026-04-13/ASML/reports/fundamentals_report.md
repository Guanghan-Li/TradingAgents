## Executive Summary

ASML Holding N.V. demonstrates exceptional fundamental strength with robust revenue growth, industry-leading margins, and dominant market positioning in extreme ultraviolet (EUV) lithography systems. ## Risk Factors

### Operational Risks
1. **Customer Concentration:** Heavy reliance on TSMC, Samsung, Intel for revenue
2. **Geopolitical Exposure:** China export restrictions impact ~15-20% of revenue
3. **Long Manufacturing Cycles:** 12-18 month lead times create inventory risk
4. **Technology Transition Risk:** High-NA EUV adoption pace uncertain

### Financial Risks
1. **Valuation Risk:** Forward PE of 32.9x leaves limited margin for disappointment
2. **Working Capital Volatility:** Large swings in deferred revenue create cash flow lumpiness
3. **Currency Exposure:** Euro-based company with global revenue (beta 1.38)

### Market Risks
1. **Semiconductor Cyclicality:** Industry historically experiences 3-4 year cycles
2. **China Demand Uncertainty:** Ongoing export control evolution
3. **Competitive Threats:** Potential alternative lithography technologies (though unlikely near-term)
