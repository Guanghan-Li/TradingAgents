## EQIX Scenario And Catalyst View

EQIX screens as a high-quality AI/data-center infrastructure compounder, but the setup is valuation-sensitive. The bullish case is supported by recent AI-native Fabric Intelligence launch momentum, analyst target increases to $1,200-$1,220, strong free cash flow of about $2.83B, and durable colocation/interconnection demand. The main offset is a premium valuation, with TTM P/E near 76.6x, forward P/E near 59.8x, price/book near 7.3x, and leverage at roughly 160% debt/equity, making the equity exposed to long-rate moves and execution misses.

Base case probability is highest: EQIX continues to benefit from AI workload growth and enterprise hybrid-cloud demand, but much of that upside appears reflected in the share price relative to the 52-week range and moving averages. Bear risk rises if rates reprice higher, AI demand monetization disappoints, or leverage/capex pressure compresses AFFO and valuation multiples.
