**Company Profile**
MAIN is Main Street Capital Corporation, a Financial Services company in Asset Management. Fundamentally, it behaves more like an internally managed business development company: earnings are driven by interest income on portfolio investments, fair-value marks, realized gains/losses on securities, and balance-sheet leverage. That matters because reported net income and margins can be volatile quarter to quarter as investment marks move, even when core portfolio cash generation is steadier.
