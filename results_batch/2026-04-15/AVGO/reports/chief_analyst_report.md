The bull case probably wins over the next 12 to 24 months if hyperscaler AI programs keep ramping and the software segment continues to anchor the model. But at today’s price, AVGO is no longer just a quality compounder; it is a quality compounder with elevated expectations. The mistake to avoid in names like AVGO is confusing a great business with a good immediate entry. At the same time, the opposite mistake is treating overbought as broken. AVGO is not broken. That keeps you aligned with the strongest parts of the AVGO thesis while respecting that, in this specific debate, timing matters more than admiration.
