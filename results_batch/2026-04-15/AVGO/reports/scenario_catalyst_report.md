## AVGO Scenario Summary

AVGO screens as a high-quality but expectation-heavy AI/semis platform: margins and free cash flow are strong, forward EPS implies a sharp earnings step-up, but the stock is also carrying a rich multiple and elevated sensitivity to execution on AI custom silicon and VMware monetization. Recent company-specific news flow is supportive, especially the expanded Meta AI relationship and broader AI chip/customer momentum, which tilts near-term setup constructive but not without valuation risk.

### Bull Case: 45%
AVGO extends its AI winner status as announced hyperscaler relationships convert into clearer revenue scale, margin resilience stays intact, and forward EPS support makes the current valuation look less stretched on a 12-month view. The key signposts are raised guidance, stronger AI/custom silicon commentary, and evidence VMware/Tanzu is stabilizing or expanding enterprise monetization.

### Base Case: 35%
AVGO executes well enough to justify continued premium status, but upside is more incremental because much of the AI optimism is already reflected in the stock. In this path, AI demand remains healthy, software contributes steadily, and macro stays non-destructive, but valuation expansion is limited and returns rely more on earnings growth than multiple rerating.

### Bear Case: 20%
The main risk is not balance-sheet stress or margin collapse; it is expectations compression. If AI program ramps slip, a major customer digests capacity, VMware integration/monetization underwhelms, or rates move higher on sticky inflation, AVGO could de-rate materially because the stock is priced for continued strong execution. Watch for weaker guide, customer concentration concerns, or signs that forward EPS expectations were too aggressive.

### Invalidation Logic
Bull is invalidated by evidence that AI/custom silicon wins are not converting into revenue and that forward EPS uplift is stalling. Base is invalidated by either a sharp upside break in AI monetization that forces estimate revisions higher or a simultaneous earnings/multiple reset from weaker demand plus tighter financial conditions. Bear is invalidated by sustained raised guidance, continued customer expansion, and stable-to-easing rate expectations that preserve the premium multiple.
