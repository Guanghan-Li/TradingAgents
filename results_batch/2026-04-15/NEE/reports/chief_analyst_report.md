So the final committee stance is Hold NEE with a Neutral relative stance. Retain exposure because the base case is still favorable enough and the long-term structure is intact, but do not overweight NEE until price strength, earnings confirmation, financing credibility, and real project conversion make the bull case more than a 30% scenario.
