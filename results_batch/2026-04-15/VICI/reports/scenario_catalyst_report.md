## VICI Scenario And Catalyst Summary

VICI screens as an income-led REIT setup rather than a high-growth equity story: dividend yield is 6.29%, forward P/E is 9.5x, P/B is 1.08x, beta is 0.73, and profitability remains high with a 69.3% net margin. The central debate is whether lower-rate duration support and continued dividend growth offset REIT multiple pressure from the 10-year Treasury at 4.26% and company-specific concentration/tenant-risk concerns.

Base case is highest probability: stable rent collection, modest tenant diversification progress, and continued dividend credibility support a range-bound to moderately positive outcome. Bull case needs lower yields, resilient casino/experiential demand, and AFFO growth visibility. Bear case is mainly driven by higher long-end rates, weaker tenant credit perception, or a dividend-growth slowdown.
