## SCCO Scenario & Catalyst Summary

SCCO is a high-quality copper producer with very strong profitability, including ~54.5% operating margin, ~32.3% net margin, ~42.7% ROE, and ~$3.34B TTM free cash flow. The main tension is not business quality but valuation and cyclicality: SCCO trades at ~36.4x TTM earnings, ~30.7x forward earnings, and ~14.2x book after a large one-year advance, leaving the stock sensitive to copper prices, project execution, analyst revisions, and macro risk appetite.

Base case is a constructive but valuation-aware consolidation scenario. Bull case requires copper strength, earnings upgrades, and continued investor willingness to pay a scarcity premium for large copper exposure. Bear case is driven by copper price reversal, inflation/rate pressure, or disappointment versus elevated expectations.
