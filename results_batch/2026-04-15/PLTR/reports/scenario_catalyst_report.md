## PLTR Scenario Summary

PLTR screens as a high-quality but high-expectation software name: profitability and cash generation are strong (`~36.3%` net margin, `~40.9%` operating margin, `~$1.26B` free cash flow), but valuation remains extreme (`~229x` TTM P/E, `~76x` forward P/E, `~46x` price/book). That creates an asymmetric setup where continued execution can support the stock, but even modest growth disappointment or multiple compression can produce sharp downside. News flow in the last 10 days is polarized, with bullish attention on Palantir's role in U.S. defense/war-room analytics and bearish attention on valuation risk and short theses.

### Bull / Base / Bear

- **Bull case (30%)**: PLTR sustains premium growth and margin expansion as U.S. government/defense AI demand deepens and commercial AI adoption remains sticky. In this case, investors look through the headline multiple because forward EPS growth catches up faster than expected.
- **Base case (45%)**: Core execution remains solid, but the stock behaves as a “good company / expensive stock.” Revenue and earnings beat modestly, yet upside is capped by valuation sensitivity and a still-positive real-rate backdrop.
- **Bear case (25%)**: Any slowdown in bookings, backlog conversion, commercial ramp, or government award timing triggers a larger derating because the starting valuation leaves little room for error. A rise in long-duration discount rates would amplify that downside.

### Key Signposts

- Government revenue growth and size/timing of defense-related awards
- Commercial customer additions, expansion rates, and AI platform monetization
- Next earnings print versus elevated expectations, especially guidance rather than backward-looking beats
- Evidence that forward EPS growth is closing the gap with the current multiple
- Rate backdrop: PLTR is especially exposed to duration compression if 10Y yields keep pushing above the current `~4.30%`

### Invalidation Logic

- **Bull invalidates** if PLTR posts only modest growth while the valuation stays stretched, or if government momentum does not translate into measurable revenue acceleration
- **Base invalidates** if PLTR either breaks into a clearly stronger growth regime with upward estimate revisions, or instead shows clear deceleration that forces a sharper rerating
- **Bear invalidates** if PLTR delivers repeated beat-and-raise quarters with strong commercial conversion and durable margin expansion, making the current forward multiple look less extreme
