## AMD Scenario And Catalyst Map

AMD screens as a high-beta AI semiconductor compounder with valuation already discounting a sharp earnings step-up: TTM P/E is ~99x versus forward P/E ~24x, implying the market expects EPS to expand materially from $2.60 TTM toward forward EPS of ~$10.83. The central scenario is not whether AMD participates in AI infrastructure, but whether MI450, EPYC share gains, and accelerator attach can convert into durable margin expansion quickly enough to justify a ~$421B market cap.

Base case probability is highest: AMD continues to gain data-center relevance but remains execution- and timing-sensitive versus Nvidia. Bull case requires clear evidence that MI450 meaningfully narrows the competitive gap and that hyperscaler demand translates into upward estimate revisions. Bear case risk is mostly valuation compression if AI revenue ramps slower than expected, gross margin leverage disappoints, or macro rates/yields keep pressure on long-duration semiconductor multiples.
