## MP Scenario Summary

MP is a high-beta, policy-sensitive rare-earth supply-chain equity where the investment case is less about current profitability and more about execution, domestic supply-chain strategic value, and NdPr / magnet pricing. Fundamentals are stretched versus current earnings power: EPS TTM is negative, EBITDA and free cash flow are negative, and forward P/E is about 47.5x, while the stock has already appreciated materially over the last year. The balance sheet has liquidity support, with a current ratio above 7x, but valuation leaves limited room for execution slippage.

**Base case probability: 50%**: MP remains strategically important, but near-term valuation consolidates while investors wait for proof of downstream magnet / separated-products scale-up and improved margins.

**Bull case probability: 30%**: policy support, Western minerals-club momentum, and customer/offtake progress re-rate MP as a scarce U.S. rare-earth platform despite weak trailing profitability.

**Bear case probability: 20%**: negative FCF, weak realized pricing, execution delays, or reduced policy premium expose the gap between valuation and current earnings.
