Actionable trading read: favor `BND` as a portfolio ballast over lower-quality yield-chasing products. The live context included interest in AAA CLO income trades, but for macro defense and cross-asset diversification, `BND` is the cleaner instrument. If the current equity melt-up continues, `BND` still offers income. If the risk rally breaks, `BND` should help on relative performance. That asymmetry is why the committee-level stance should be `BUY` for `BND`, especially on weakness.
