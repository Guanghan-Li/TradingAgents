Strongest bearish signal: valuation stretch under slowing growth. This is the main counterweight and likely the most important risk-control rule because it is also high weight. Key conflict: the rule set is evenly split between a high-conviction growth bull case and a high-conviction valuation bear case. If market, news, or fundamentals evidence shows backlog and margin resilience, the bullish rule has more weight.
