## ASML Scenario Summary

ASML remains a high-quality monopoly-like lithography asset with strong profitability and cash generation, but the setup is now a timing question between AI-driven demand strength and China/export-control drag. The core tension in the current tape is that Q1 2026 was fundamentally solid and guidance was lifted, yet the stock still sold off on concerns that China demand is rolling over and that a 51x trailing / 32x forward multiple leaves little room for execution misses.

- **Bull case (30%)**: AI-led foundry and memory capex more than offsets China weakness, backlog/order quality stays strong, and the raised 2026 outlook proves conservative. In this case, ASML can sustain premium valuation despite rate sensitivity.
- **Base case (50%)**: Non-China demand stays healthy enough to support the new 2026 outlook, but export restrictions cap upside and keep sentiment choppy. The stock likely trades as a high-quality but fully-valued compounder.
- **Bear case (20%)**: China restrictions create a larger-than-expected revenue/mix hole, new orders do not fully backfill, and the market de-rates the name as a cyclical semi-equipment stock rather than a scarcity asset.

### Key signposts
- Whether post-Q1 order momentum confirms that AI demand is broad enough to replace China-related weakness.
- Any further evidence that export bans are worsening revenue mix, service revenue, or shipment timing.
- Whether long-duration valuation pressure increases if inflation remains firm and long yields keep drifting higher.

### Thesis invalidation logic
- Bull case is invalidated if China weakness spreads into broader order softness or management walks back the raised 2026 outlook.
- Base case is invalidated if either AI-driven orders accelerate far beyond expectations or export-control damage materially worsens.
- Bear case is invalidated if backlog conversion and bookings show that restricted-China demand is being replaced cleanly by leading-edge AI/foundry customers.
