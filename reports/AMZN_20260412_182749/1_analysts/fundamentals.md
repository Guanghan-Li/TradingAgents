## Financial Performance Analysis

### Revenue Trends
Amazon demonstrates **strong sequential revenue growth**:
- **Q4 2025:** $213.4B (quarterly record)
- **Q3 2025:** $180.2B
- **Q2 2025:** $167.7B
- **Q1 2025:** $155.7B
- **Q4 2024:** $187.8B

**Key Insight:** The company achieved 13.8% QoQ growth from Q3 to Q4 2025, significantly outpacing typical seasonal patterns. TTM revenue reached $716.9B, reflecting a healthy revenue base with strong AWS contribution.

### Profitability Expansion

**Net Income Trend (Quarterly):**
- **Q4 2025:** $21.2B (+0.02% vs Q3)
- **Q3 2025:** $21.2B (+16.6% vs Q2)
- **Q2 2025:** $18.2B (+6.1% vs Q1)
- **Q1 2025:** $17.1B (-14.4% vs Q4 2024)
- **Q4 2024:** $20.0B

**Key Insight:** Amazon has achieved **consistent $17-21B quarterly net income levels**, demonstrating stabilized profitability. The Q3 spike represents strong operational performance.

**Margin Analysis:**
- **Gross Profit Margin (TTM):** 50.3% (strong pricing power)
- **Operating Margin (TTM):** 10.5% (improving from historical 7-9%)
- **Net Profit Margin (TTM):** 10.8% (excellent for retail/cloud hybrid)
- **Return on Equity (TTM):** 22.3% (exceptional returns for shareholders)
- **Return on Assets (TTM):** 6.9% (improving asset efficiency)

**EBITDA Performance:**
- **Q4 2025 EBITDA:** $46.8B (Normalized: $45.5B)
- **Q3 2025 EBITDA:** $45.5B (Normalized: $35.3B)
- **TTM EBITDA:** $145.7B

**Critical Finding:** Q3 2025 shows unusual items ($10.2B), likely one-time gains from equity investments. Normalizing shows consistent $33-37B quarterly baseline with Q4 showing genuine operational improvement to $45.5B.

### Operating Leverage
- **Operating Income Q4 2025:** $25.0B (vs $17.4B in Q3)
- **Operating Margin Trend:** Expanding from 9.7% (Q3) to 11.7% (Q4)
