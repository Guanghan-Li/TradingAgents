## Silver Demand & Supply Drivers

### Structural Supports for Silver
1. **Industrial Demand:** Silver is essential in solar panels, electronics, and renewable energy infrastructure—all benefiting from ESG tailwinds.
2. **Inflation Hedge:** Accelerating PPI (+2.08%) supports commodity prices as real yields remain low/moderate.
3. **Portfolio Diversification:** With equities and bonds both volatile, precious metals provide uncorrelated exposure.

### Headwinds
1. **Stable/Non-Accommodative Rates:** Fed holding at 3.64% reduces the opportunity cost of holding non-yielding silver.
2. **Risk-On Environment:** Falling VIX suggests lower safe-haven demand.
3. **Dollar Strength:** Not explicitly measured here, but strong USD typically pressures commodity prices.
