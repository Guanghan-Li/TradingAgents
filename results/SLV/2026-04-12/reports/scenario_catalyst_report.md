## Scenario Map

**Thesis:** Geopolitical risk (Middle East ceasefire fragility) + falling USD + record retail macro engagement drive safe-haven demand. Technical breakout above $100 signals momentum.

**Valuation:** P/B 3.24 justified; SLV targets $100–$110 (52-week high).

- Geopolitical escalation (ceasefire breakdown)

**Valuation:** P/B normalizes to 2.5–2.8; price drifts toward 50-day MA ($72.49).

**Thesis:** Economic slowdown (rising unemployment, +2.08% PPI pressure) triggers >15% equity drawdown. Forced commodity liquidations & margin calls drain SLV ETF outflows despite initial flight-to-safety.

**Valuation:** P/B compresses to 1.5–2.0; SLV crashes to $28–$50 (52-week low or below).

**Key Signposts:**
- S&P 500 declines >10%
- VIX spikes >30
- Unemployment rises >4.5%
- PPI persists; Fed forced to hike >4%
