**Segment Analyst Report: Tesla Inc. (TSLA)**

**Executive Summary**
Tesla's business remains heavily automotive-dependent (~86% revenue) with acute margin compression (gross margin ~16% Q1 2025 vs. 18% TTM). Operating leverage deteriorated sharply (operating margin 2.6% Q1 vs. 6.2% Q4 2024), reflecting pricing pressure and elevated R&D spend on FSD and Semi. Energy Storage (8% revenue) offers margin upside but is insufficient to offset auto headwinds. Current valuation (127x forward PE) leaves no room for earnings misses. Near-term downside risk dominates; medium-term catalysts (compact SUV, margin recovery, energy scaling) are execution-dependent.

**FINAL TRANSACTION PROPOSAL: HOLD** — Elevated downside risk outweighs optionality pending Q1 2025 guidance confirmation and compact SUV demand signals.
