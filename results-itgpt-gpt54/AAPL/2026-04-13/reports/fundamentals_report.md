# Comprehensive Fundamental Analysis: Apple Inc. (AAPL)
**Report Date:** April 13, 2026

## Executive Summary

Apple Inc. remains a dominant force in consumer electronics with a market capitalization of $3.83 trillion. The company demonstrates strong profitability with a 27% net margin and robust cash generation, though recent performance shows underperformance relative to semiconductor peers and mixed signals on growth momentum.

## Company Profile

- **Sector:** Technology

- **Market Cap:** $3.83 trillion

## Financial Performance Analysis

### Revenue & Profitability (Q1 FY2026 - Q4 FY2025)

Apple delivered **$143.8B in revenue for Q1 FY2026** (Dec 2025), representing the strongest quarter in the trailing period and reflecting typical holiday seasonality. Sequential quarterly revenue progression shows:

- Q1 FY2026 (Dec '25): $143.8B
- Q4 FY2025 (Sep '25): $102.5B  
- Q3 FY2025 (Jun '25): $94.0B
- Q2 FY2025 (Mar '25): $95.4B
- Q1 FY2025 (Dec '24): $124.3B

**Year-over-year Q1 comparison:** Revenue grew 15.7% from $124.3B to $143.8B, demonstrating solid holiday demand.

**Profitability metrics remain exceptional:**
- Gross margin: 48.2% (Q1 FY2026)
- Operating margin: 35.4% (TTM)
- Net margin: 27.0% (TTM)
- Net income Q1 FY2026: $42.1B (29.3% margin)

The company generated **$117.8B in net income (TTM)**, translating to $7.89 EPS with forward guidance suggesting $9.31 EPS.

### Margin Trend Analysis

Gross profit margins have remained stable in the 46-48% range across quarters:
- Q1 FY2026: 48.2%
- Q4 FY2025: 47.2%
- Q3 FY2025: 46.5%
- Q2 FY2025: 47.1%

Operating expenses as % of revenue show disciplined cost management at 12.8% in Q1 FY2026, with R&D spending of $10.9B (7.6% of revenue) indicating continued innovation investment.
