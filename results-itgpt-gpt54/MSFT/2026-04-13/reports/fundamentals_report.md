# Microsoft Corporation (MSFT) Fundamental Analysis Report
**Report Date:** April 13, 2026

## Executive Summary

Microsoft Corporation demonstrates robust fundamental strength with a market capitalization of $2.76 trillion, though the stock has significantly underperformed its technology peers over recent periods. The company maintains exceptional profitability metrics with a 39% profit margin and 47% operating margin, supported by strong free cash flow generation of $53.6 billion TTM. However, the stock's -21.4% YTD performance and -29% six-month decline warrant careful examination of valuation and competitive positioning.

## Company Profile

**Sector:** Technology  
**Industry:** Software - Infrastructure  
**Market Cap:** $2.76 trillion  
**Beta:** 1.107

Microsoft operates as a diversified technology company providing software, services, devices, and solutions globally. The company's business segments span cloud computing (Azure), productivity software (Office 365), gaming (Xbox), and enterprise solutions.

The dramatic 60% YoY net income growth in the most recent quarter significantly outpaces revenue growth, indicating strong operating leverage and margin expansion.

**Capital Expenditure Acceleration:**
- Q1 FY2026: $29.9B (37% of revenue)

The CapEx intensity has compressed free cash flow to $5.9B in Q1 FY2026 from $25.7B in Q4 FY2025.

**Free Cash Flow:** $53.6B TTM, though quarterly FCF volatility is high due to lumpy CapEx timing.
